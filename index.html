    ABOUT THE EVENT

    La Belle'22 is the first grand event conducted by WOW IEDC MESCE in collaboration with IEDC MES KVM ,IEDC MJPTC ,IEDC MESAC ,IEDC UNIVERSITY OF CALICUT and AMAL COLLEGE OF ADVANCED STUDIES.La Belle involves workshops, talk shows, panel discussions, cultural events, entertainment shows, competitions, games and much more spanned across two vibrant days. With LaBelle, we aim to bridge the gap between the women community in technology and entrepreneurship and to build a space for these individuals to learn and grow . Furthermore, we look forward to promoting and fostering small businesses and startup ideas among the student community.

    FAQ

    • WHAT IS WOW IEDC MESCE?

      WOW expanded as Women Of Wonder, is a subsection of the IEDC (Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell) Franchise to help, support, and inspire all the wonderful women in Tech and Business to achieve their goals. WOW was officially formed in 2018 with the motive to help the students of MESCE with their creative ideas. It is to uplift the girls with technical skills and to empower them.

    • WHAT IS LA BELLE’22?

      La Belle'22 is the first grant event conducted by WOW IEDC MESCE in collaboration with IEDC MES KVM,IEDC MJPTC,IEDC MESAC,IEDC UNIVERSITY OF CALICUT and AMAL COLLEGE OF ADVANCED STUDIES. La belle involves two vibrant days with workshops, talk shows, panel discussions, cultural events, entertainment shows, competitions, games and more. With La belle, we aim to connect the women's community to technology and entrepreneurship. Furthermore, we look forward to promoting and fostering small businesses and startup ideas among the student community. Become a fire of success with La belle.

    • WHO CAN ATTEND LA BELLE’22?

      Absolutely open to all! No matter what course you're in, La Belle'22 would be worth attending!

    • IS IEDC MEMBERSHIP MANDATORY?

      No. Any student can register and be a part of LaBelle’22

    • DO YOU PROVIDE AN ACCOMMODATION FACILITY?

      Yes. We'll be arranging accommodation facilities for boys and girls in our respective hostels.
